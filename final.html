Our Services

Sisterlocks Installations

Sisterlocks are a unique and versatile hairstyle that originated in the early 1990s, designed specifically for those with naturally curly or textured hair. Developed by Dr. JoAnne Cornwell, Sisterlocks combine the beauty of locs with a lightweight and customizable approach, allowing individuals to embrace their natural hair while enjoying the benefits of a stylish and low-maintenance look. Unlike traditional locs, Sisterlocks use a specialized locking technique that creates smaller, uniform locs, providing a more refined appearance.

With extensive training and experience, our team specializes in precise Sisterlocks installation, ensuring beautiful, healthy locs tailored to your unique style.

Loc Maintenance

Comprehensive loc care to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Caring for Sisterlocks is like nurturing a garden; regular attention and love ensure they flourish beautifully. Consistent washing, moisturizing, and gentle retightening keep them healthy and vibrant. Embrace the journey, as patience pays off in stunning, well-maintained locs that reflect your unique style and personality. Celebrate your natural beauty daily!

Re-ties are done between 6-8 Weeks. We use a 4 or 3 point rotation to ensure that locs loc the right way.
